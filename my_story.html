My Story

Video Games have been a part of my life for a long time; since I was a kid. They have been a way to connect and spend my free time with my friends and family. Often losing track of time being immersed in the world of the game. Video Games captivate me because of all that comes together to make the game. Like the code and technology working behind the scenes to make the game functional. There was a time in seventh grade when my math teacher linked us to a website where we could code anything we wanted. Which sparked something in me where I realized that this captivation for Video Games has led me to find an interest in technology and coding.
